Publication(s)

Caleb Rotello, Eric B. Jones, Peter Graf, Eliot Kapit
February, 2023
Automated detection of symmetry-protected subspaces in quantum simulations

We show that, starting from a chosen basis, any invariant, symmetry-protected subspaces which are diagonal in that basis are discoverable using transitive closure on graphs representing state-to-state interactions under k-local unitary operations.
